The Artist

Meet the Artist

With over a decade of experience in luxury bridal beauty, I've had the privilege of preparing hundreds of brides for the most photographed moment of their lives. My approach blends timeless technique with modern artistry — creating looks that are polished, intentional, and built to last from the first dance to the final goodbye.

I specialize in bridal makeup that enhances rather than transforms — because the goal is always for you to look like the most radiant version of yourself. Whether your vision is soft and romantic, bold and editorial, or somewhere beautifully in between, I'll craft a look tailored entirely to you.

Based in Las Vegas and available for destination weddings worldwide, I bring a full professional kit, a calm presence, and years of on-location experience to every booking — from intimate elopements to grand ballroom affairs.
